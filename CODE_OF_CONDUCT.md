# Code of Conduct

CodeMaster aims to be a practical, respectful and welcoming engineering community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but be clear when something is unsafe or incorrect.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and user impact.
- Provide reproducible examples when reporting bugs.
- Respect maintainers' time and review bandwidth.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Repeated off-topic comments.
- Threats, intimidation or personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide or remove comments, close issues, block users or take other reasonable action to keep the project healthy.

If you see a problem, contact the maintainers privately.
